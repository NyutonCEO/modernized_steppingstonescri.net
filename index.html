Services We Provide

About Stepping Stones

Stepping Stones provides high-quality addiction recovery services with a focus on individualized care, evidence-based treatment, and whole-person renewal. Our team helps clients and families navigate the journey from crisis to stability with dignity and hope.

How Our Program Works

Step 1: Reach Out

Call our helpline or fill out a form — confidential support is available 24/7.

Step 2: Assessment & Detox

Comprehensive clinical evaluation and safe, medically supervised detoxification when needed.

Step 3: Personalized Treatment

Individual therapy, group sessions, and evidence-based clinical care tailored to your goals.

Step 4: Aftercare Support

Structured follow-up, alumni groups, ongoing counseling, and community support.
