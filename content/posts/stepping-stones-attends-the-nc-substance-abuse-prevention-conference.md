<p><strong>Stepping Stones Community Resources exhibited and attended the 13 Annual North Carolina Substance Abuse Prevention Conference, May 2-3, 2017 at the McKimmon Conference and Training Center in Raleigh NC.</strong></p>
<p>This conference covered so many issues facing North Carolina. Some of the topics discussed were:</p>
<p>&#8220;What is Big Marijuana and Where Are We Going with Pot Policy in the US?&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;Statewide Collaboration Increases the Impact of Prevention&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;Is Childhood Adversity the Real Gateway Drug?&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;Everything You Need to Know About Recovery High Schools&#8221;</p>
<p>On the first day of the conference, The Honorable Josh Stein, Attorney General of North Carolina, spoke on &#8220;Confronting the Opioid Epidemic in North Carolina&#8221;. In addition, Erin J. Day, BA, BS, Director of the Wilson County Substance Abuse Coalition, presented a breakout session on &#8220;Rx Media Campaign: Through the Local Looking Glass&#8221;.</p>
<p>A very informative and thought-provoking conference.</p>
