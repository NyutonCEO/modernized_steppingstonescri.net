Human Rights Committee

Stepping Stones Community Resources, Inc. provides a Human Rights Committee which meets quarterly. This committee discusses the rights of persons served and assists in reviewing and/or updating current policies and procedures of persons served rights. The committee’s main objective is protecting the rights of persons served at Stepping Stones Community Resources, Inc. and is made up of past and current persons served, stakeholders and staff members. The committee meetings are held at 3904-A Airport Drive, Wilson NC and lunch will be served.
