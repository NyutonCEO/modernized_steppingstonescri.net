Did you get a DWI or a Criminal Drug Charge? Well, you’ve come to the right place!


Stepping Stones Community Resources, Inc., “FIX MY DWI” Services has a state-licensed facility in Wilson, NC, to perform DWI and Substance Abuse Assessments, evaluate the results and provide recommended DWI or Substance Abuse treatment.

A DWI Assessment, also known as an Alcohol Assessment, is required of anyone who is convicted of DWI in the state of North Carolina. You cannot receive a Limited Driving Privilege without a DWI Assessment.

We also provide Out-Of-State Reviews.

If you receive a criminal drug charge, whether a misdemeanor or felony, the court will more than likely require you to get a Substance Abuse Assessment. We provide the assessment, evaluate the results, and provided recommended Substance Abuse treatment.

****Obtaining a proper assessment and treatment for a DWI or a criminal drug charge can potentially help you if obtained ahead of your court date.

We have Certified Substance Abuse Counselors to guide you through your treatment.

Treatment Options:

Alcohol & Drug Education Traffic School (ADETS)  $160

ADETS is an educational program for someone with a DWI conviction. This program is for people who don’t have an identified substance use disorder but may be at risk for these disorders.

Short-Term Treatment  20/30

Short-term substance abuse treatment consists of 20 hours of counseling covered over a minimum of 30 days. First-Time Offenders receive individual and group counseling on chemical dependency issues.

Long-Term Treatment 40/60

Long-term substance abuse treatment consists of 40 hours of counseling covered over a minimum of 60 days. Includes group and individual counseling over a longer period of time.

90 Day Treatment

Intensive outpatient treatment including group and individual counseling.

120 Day Treatment

Intensive outpatient treatment including group and individual counseling.
