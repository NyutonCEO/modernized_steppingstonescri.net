Behavioral/Mental Health

Confidential care. No referral required.

Behavioral/Mental Health affects how we think, feel, and act. It includes our emotional, mental, and overall well-being. It also controls how we handle stress, make choices, and relate to others. Your thinking, mood, and behavior could be affected if you experience mental health problems.

There are many causes that can play apart in behavioral/mental health problems, including:

  • Family history of mental health problems
  • Biological factors, such as genes or brain chemistry
  • Life experiences, such as trauma or abuse

Behavioral/Mental Health problems are universal and we can help you get better and set you on a path of complete recovery.
