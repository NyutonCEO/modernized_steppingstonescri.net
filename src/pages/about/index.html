Stepping Stones Community Resources, Inc. provides compassionate outpatient care for people seeking help with mental health, substance use, and life challenges in Wilson, North Carolina.

Our mission

Stepping Stones Community Resources, Inc. is committed to providing needed services to those in need and their families who are struggling with behavioral/mental and substance use disorders. We promote increased quality of life through strength based intervention and Person Centered Planning. We believe in transforming everyone's potential into reality.

Our approach

We meet you with respect, listen carefully, and build a plan around your needs. Our team focuses on practical next steps, coordinated support, and clear communication so you always know what to expect.

Who we serve

We serve adolescents, adults, men, and women seeking help with substance use, mental health challenges, behavioral concerns, or DWI-related requirements.

Services we offer

  • Individual Outpatient Therapy for stress, mood, and life challenges.
  • Substance Abuse Counseling (SACOT) focused on recovery skills and stability.
  • Substance Abuse Intensive Outpatient Program (SAIOP) with structured support.
  • Case Management connecting you to resources and practical support.
  • Primary Care supporting whole-person wellness.
  • DWI Services for assessments and education.
