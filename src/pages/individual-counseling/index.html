Individual Outpatient Therapy

Individual Outpatient Therapy is a specialized treatment that is personalized to the needs of the individual and recognizes the importance of the individual. We provide quality treatment in areas of anger management, mood disorders, anxiety disorders, attention deficit hyperactivity disorders (ADHD), behavioral disorders, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), stress management, substance abuse disorders, gambling addiction disorders, eating disorders, and obsessive-compulsive disorders.
